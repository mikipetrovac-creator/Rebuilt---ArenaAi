Why Side works as a base

Side lies about 60 km west of Alanya, and that changes the arithmetic of a day trip. Pamukkale and the Lycian coast around Demre are noticeably closer from here, so you spend less of the day on the bus. Green Canyon is closer still — the Oymapınar reservoir sits just above Manavgat, a short drive inland. Cappadocia is the exception: it lies far to the northeast, which is why we run it over two days with an overnight stay rather than squeezing it into one.

FAQ

How much do excursions from Side cost in 2026?

Green Canyon starts at €30 per adult, Pamukkale at €40 and Demre-Myra-Kekova at €55. The 2-day Cappadocia tour starts at €35 per adult in the budget package. Children pay a reduced rate and infants under 3 travel free.

Which resorts around Side do you pick up from?

Free hotel pickup covers Side centre, Manavgat, Çolaklı, Kızılağaç, Evrenseki, Kumköy, Titreyengöl, Sorgun and Gündoğdu. The exact time is confirmed on WhatsApp the evening before.

What is the best short excursion from Side?

Green Canyon. The Oymapınar reservoir sits just above Manavgat, roughly half an hour from Side, so pickup is late morning and you are back the same afternoon — the most relaxed day we run.

Can I join the Cappadocia tour starting from Side?

Yes. The 2-day Cappadocia tour picks up from Side hotels on the same schedule as Alanya — Mondays, Thursdays and Saturdays. The distance is exactly why it is a 2-day tour with an overnight stay.

Do I have to pay online in advance?

No. You send a booking request through the site, we confirm availability and your pickup time on WhatsApp, and you pay on the day of the tour.
