ABOUT THE DESTINATION

Journey to the Sunken City and the Home of Saint Nicholas

The ancient Lycian coast stretches along Turkey's stunning Mediterranean shoreline, hiding beneath its turquoise waters the ruins of cities swallowed by earthquakes millennia ago. Kekova's Sunken City, the rock-carved tombs of Myra, and the church of St. Nicholas — the real-life inspiration for Santa Claus — make this one of the most historically rich day trips in all of Turkey.

Our carefully designed full-day tour takes you from the comfort of your hotel in Alanya or Side through dramatic coastal scenery to three UNESCO-worthy landmarks, with a refreshing swim stop in a crystal-clear bay along the way.

⛵ The Sunken City of Kekova

Special Highlight • Boat Cruise

The Sunken City of Kekova (ancient Dolchiste) was a thriving Lycian settlement that was partially submerged beneath the Mediterranean following a devastating 2nd-century earthquake. Today, the remarkably preserved ruins — staircases, walls, and foundations — are clearly visible through the crystal-clear turquoise water from the boat's glass bottom.

During the 2-hour cruise, you'll glide over the submerged city, pass by the medieval fortress of Simena (Kaleköy), and enjoy a swim stop in a pristine turquoise bay surrounded by pine-covered hills.

📍 Location: Kekova Island, Demre District, Antalya
🕐 Duration: Approximately 2 hours
✓ Included: In Premium package / Budget: ticket paid on-site

🏛 Lycian Rock Tombs & Roman Theatre — Myra

Special Highlight • Ancient Ruins

The ancient city of Myra was one of the six leading cities of the Lycian League. Its most spectacular feature is the collection of extraordinary rock-cut tombs carved directly into the sheer vertical cliff face — each one an intricate, temple-like façade that once housed the Lycian elite.

Adjacent to the tombs stands a magnificently preserved Roman amphitheatre with a capacity of 13,000 spectators, one of the finest examples of Greco-Roman architecture on the Mediterranean coast.

📍 Location: Demre (ancient Myra), Antalya Province
🕐 Duration: Approximately 45–60 minutes
✓ Included: In Premium package / Budget: entry paid on-site
×

⛪ St. Nicholas Church — Demre

Special Highlight • Historical Pilgrimage

This ancient pilgrimage church was built over the tomb of St. Nicholas of Myra, the historical 4th-century bishop whose legendary generosity inspired the global figure of Santa Claus. The church dates from the 6th century and contains beautiful Byzantine-era floor mosaics and frescoes.

Inside, visitors can see the original marble sarcophagus where St. Nicholas was first buried, along with stunning architectural details that reveal centuries of Christian worship on this sacred site.

📍 Location: Demre (ancient Myra), Antalya Province
🕐 Duration: Approximately 30–45 minutes
✓ Included: In Premium package / Budget: entry paid on-site
