FAQ

How much do excursions from Alanya cost in 2026?

Our day tours start at €30 per adult for Green Canyon, €40 for Pamukkale and €55 for Demre-Myra-Kekova. The 2-day Cappadocia tour starts at €35 per adult in the budget package. Children pay less and infants under 3 travel free.

Do you pick up from Side as well as Alanya?

Yes. All our tours include free hotel pickup from Alanya, Side and the resorts in between, including Konaklı, Avsallar, Okurcalar, Mahmutlar and Kestel.

Do I have to pay online in advance?

No. You send a booking request through the site, we confirm availability and your pickup time on WhatsApp, and you pay on the day of the tour.

Which excursion is best if I only have one day?

Pamukkale and Demre-Myra-Kekova are the two classic full-day trips. Green Canyon is a more relaxed day on the water. Cappadocia needs two days because of the distance.

Not sure which tour to pick?

Write to us on WhatsApp and we'll help you choose based on how many days you have and who is travelling with you.
